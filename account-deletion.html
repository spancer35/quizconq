Account Deletion - QuizConq

Last Updated: April 2026

You can request account deletion directly inside the QuizConq app. Open the account panel and use the delete account option.

How to delete your account in-app

  1. Open QuizConq and sign in.
  2. Go to the account panel from the start screen.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the action in the final confirmation dialog.

What gets deleted

  • Account record used for login and profile.
  • In-game profile data linked to your account.
  • Associated gameplay progress stored under your user id.

Retention and legal exceptions

Some minimal technical logs may be retained for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance for a limited period where required.
